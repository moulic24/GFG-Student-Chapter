Ideathon (Agri-Hack)

Date : 14-15th September 2022

Time : 9:00 PM â€“ 4:00PM

Where was the event : On-campus

Location :EF-04

No.of teams registered : 35

Event Highlights:

1.Participation:

GeeksforGeeks VIIT Team successfully conducted the Agri-Hack Ideathon, attracting 35 enthusiastic teams, each consisting of 4 members from different colleges in the regions of Visakhapatnam and Vizianagaram. The event provided a unique platform for participants to showcase their innovative ideas in the field of agriculture and technology.

2.Idea Presentations:

Participating teams presented their creative and groundbreaking ideas related to the Agri-Hack theme. The ideas ranged from AI-driven smart farming solutions to sustainable agriculture practices.

3.Certification of Participation:

Every participant received a Certificate of Participation, recognizing their active involvement and contribution to the ideathon.

4.Appreciation Certificates for Winners:

The top-performing teams were awarded Appreciation Certificates for their outstanding ideas and presentations. Their contributions were acknowledged for their potential to make a positive impact on the agriculture sector.

5.Cash Prizes Distribution:

In addition to certificates, cash prizes were distributed among the winning teams as a token of recognition for their exceptional efforts and ingenuity.
