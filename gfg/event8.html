Event Highlights:

1.Skill Enhancement:

GeeksforGeeks VIIT Team conducted a practical session on Competitive Coding, aimed at enhancing the coding skills of junior students. The session emphasized problem-solving techniques and algorithmic thinking.

2.Interactive Approach:

The session followed an interactive approach, engaging juniors through hands-on coding exercises and real-time problem-solving challenges.

3.Introduction to Platforms:

Juniors were introduced to popular competitive coding platforms, such as Codeforces, LeetCode, and HackerRank, to expose them to diverse coding challenges.

4.Mentorship by Seniors:

Senior members of GeeksforGeeks VIIT Team mentored the juniors, providing guidance, insights, and personal experiences in competitive coding.

5.Problem Discussions:

The session included discussions on frequently asked coding problems, where juniors had the opportunity to learn multiple approaches to tackle them.

6.Prizes and Certificates:

Participants showcased their coding skills through coding contests, and winners were awarded prizes and certificates to recognize their outstanding performance.
