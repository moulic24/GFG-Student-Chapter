Chapter Inauguration Event and Quiz

Date : 26 August 2022

Time : 2:00 PM â€“ 4:30PM

Where was the event : On-campus

Location : Visvesvaraya Hall

No.of students attended : 230

Event Highlights:

1.Lighting of the Lamp:

The inaugural ceremony commenced with the traditional lighting of the lamp, symbolizing knowledge and prosperity. Dignitaries, faculty, and students participated in the auspicious ritual.

2.Words of Wisdom:

Esteemed dignitaries, including the Principal, Rector, R&D Dean, CSE HOD, and AI&DS HOD, shared inspiring and motivating words with the audience. They emphasized the significance of technology and innovation in shaping future careers.

3.Introduction of Core Team Members:

The core team members of the GeeksforGeeks Student Chapter were introduced, comprising dedicated students passionate about technology. They expressed their commitment to fostering a vibrant tech community.

4.Brainstorming Quiz:

An interactive and engaging quiz session followed, challenging participants' knowledge on various technology domains. The quiz encouraged active participation and showcased the students' enthusiasm for learning.

5.Supportive Faculty:

Faculty members attended the event and showed their strong support for the new student chapter. They pledged to mentor and guide the students in their tech endeavors .

6.3rd Year Students' Involvement:

Enthusiastic third-year students actively participated in the event, displaying their zeal for building a thriving tech community on campus.

The inauguration of the GeeksforGeeks Student Chapter at VIIT marked the beginning of an exciting journey in the world of technology. With the backing of esteemed dignitaries, supportive faculty, and enthusiastic students, the chapter is set to flourish and create a platform for knowledge sharing, skill development, and innovation.
