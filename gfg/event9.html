Event Highlights:

The event featured fun-based activities like coding games, tech quizzes, puzzles, and group challenges, fostering a dynamic and enjoyable learning atmosphere.

Participants had the chance to attend diverse skill sessions, ranging from coding workshops and data analytics to web development and artificial intelligence.

Attendees had an exclusive opportunity to register for selected online courses on GeeksforGeeks at discounted rates, making the summer a rewarding learning experience.

The event facilitated networking and collaboration among students, fostering a sense of camaraderie and peer learning within the tech community.
