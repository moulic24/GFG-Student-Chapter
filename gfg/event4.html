Event Highlights:

1.Series of Informative Sessions:

GeeksforGeeks VIIT Team organized a series of enlightening sessions on "Mastering OS and Troubleshooting." The sessions aimed to equip students with in-depth knowledge of operating systems and effective troubleshooting techniques.

Distinguished seniors, who secured placements as Customer Support Associates (CSA) at Amazon, were invited as guest speakers. Their industry expertise added immense value to the sessions.

The sessions saw active participation from VIIT students, who showed keen interest in mastering the intricacies of operating systems and enhancing their troubleshooting skills.

4.Learning and Skill Development:

The sessions provided valuable insights into OS concepts, system architecture, and advanced troubleshooting approaches. Students gained a competitive edge and enhanced their technical prowess.

5.Gratitude to Senior CSAs:

GeeksforGeeks VIIT Team expressed their gratitude to the senior CSA speakers for generously sharing their knowledge and experiences, inspiring the next generation of tech enthusiasts.

Students had the opportunity to interact with the guest speakers, fostering meaningful networking and gaining valuable industry insights.

GeeksforGeeks VIIT Team assured students of continued support in their career endeavors, providing guidance and resources for excelling in the tech industry.

The Mastering OS and Troubleshooting Sessions by GeeksforGeeks VIIT Team served as an excellent platform for students to strengthen their technical foundation and explore exciting career prospects.
